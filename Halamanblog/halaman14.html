ACTIVE AND PASSIVE SENTENCE

Sc:-


1. Active Sentence

Active adalah kalimat yang subjeknya berbuat sesuatu atau melakukan suatu pekerjaan.

*a) Rumus Active dan Contoh Kalimat:

Rumus :
Subject + Verb (sesuai tenses) + Object

Contoh Kalimat:
=> He plays a game on his phone.
=> People call my cat 'Fat' because he is so fat.
=> My brother repaired my computer yesterday

2. Passive Sentence

Passive adalah frasa kata benda yang akan menjadi objek dari kalimat aktif.

*a) Rumus Passive dan Contoh Kalimat:

Rumus :
Subject + be + V3 (past participle) + by (doer/pelaku tindakan)

Contoh Kalimat:
=> A game on his phone is played by him.
=> My cat is called 'Fat' by people because he is so fat.
=> My computer was repaired by my brother yesterday.

3. Kesimpulan

Berdasarkan penjelasan di atas, dapat disimpulkan, bahwa active adalah suatu bentuk kalimat di mana subjek kalimat melakukan tindakan, bukan menerima tindakan.Sedangkan subjek pada passive untuk menerima tindakan.
